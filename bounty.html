Bounty Hunters

Refer projects on Ethereum and earn 33% of fees

Scroll to Learn More

Instructions

Message angelo_streamflow on Telegram and inform him of the project you’d like to refer

Once approved create a Telegram group with Streamflow and the project you want to refer

Once the referred project sets up a vesting contract with us you will automatically get paid 33% of the fees generated

Fee Examples

Project A

Averages $15,000,000 fully diluted market cap over 3 years

0.0025 (fees) x $15,000,000 FDV = $37,500 fees x 33% = $12,375 worth of tokens earned

Project B

Averages $50,000,000 fully diluted market cap over 2 years

0.0025 (fees) x $50,000,000 FDV = $125,000 fees x 33% = $41,250 worth of tokens earned

Project C

Averages $80,000,000 fully diluted market cap over 4 years

0.0025 (fees) x $80,000,000 FDV = $200,000 fees x 33% = $66,000 worth of tokens earned

Please note the following:

Our fee is collected in the token that is being vested, not USDC or similar.

The referrer will receive the 33% fee in the token that is being vested, not in USDC or similar.

The fee split does not get paid upfront immediately after the vesting contract creation. It will be distributed in a manner that follow the vesting schedule (eg. monthly) and will distribute the full fee when the vesting contract ends.

Example: Token A is being vested monthly for 3 years (36 months total), the referrer (you) will receive 2.77% of the 33% fee per month for 36 months.

Examples provided are for illustrative purposes only, this is not a financial nor investment advice. More information about the program available upon request.
